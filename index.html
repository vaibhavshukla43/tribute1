A.P.J Abdul Kalam


1931-2015

Missile man of India

The "Missile Man of India" refers to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the 11th President of India. He earned this title due to his significant contributions to India's missile technology development. Dr. Kalam played a crucial role in the development of India's ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He was not only a brilliant scientist but also a beloved public figure known for his humility, vision, and dedication to the advancement of science and technology for the betterment of society.

BIOGRAPHY

  • "Wings of Fire: An Autobiography" - This book is an autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, co-authored with Arun Tiwari.
  • "My Journey: Transforming Dreams into Actions" - Another autobiography of Dr. Kalam, co-authored with Arun Tiwari.
  • "The People's President: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam" by S.M. Khan - This biography offers a comprehensive look at Dr. Kalam's life, from his childhood to his presidency.
  • "A.P.J. Abdul Kalam: The Visionary of India" by K. Bhushan and G. Katyal - This biography delves into Dr. Kalam's life, focusing on his role as a visionary leader and his efforts to ignite young minds through education and innovation.
